K10 Changelog

This log provides a high-level summary of major contributions and development phases for the K10 project. For a more granular, iterative history, consult the project's version control logs.

Phase 5: The Unawakened World (Ongoing)

  • Primary Goal: To significantly deepen the lore and narrative significance of the Unawakened factions, particularly the guilds of Nexus. This phase will shift focus from high-level magical conflicts to the mundane, economic, and social systems that form the bedrock of the K10 world.
  • Key Activities:
    • Expanding the roster of Unawakened character exemplars and integrating them into existing narratives.
    • Detailing the internal politics, key figures, and operational methods of the major Nexus guilds.
    • Creating new narrative content (case studies, reports) that showcases the Unawakened perspective and their unique forms of power.
    • Nexus & Unawakened Deep Dive: Completed several major cycles focused on Phase 5. This involved creating new Unawakened lore and character profiles (Master Caelus, The Oracle, Somnus), adding descriptive text for visual assets (Astral Pests, Beast-Kin), and validating guild mechanics via playtesting. Significant review and maintenance passes were performed to consolidate historical documents (e.g., factional schisms), improve prose, fix a large volume of broken links, and resolve issues with the catalog generation script.
  • Cultural Expansion: Completed a full development cycle focused on expanding factional culture. This involved creating dedicated documents for architectural-styles.md, factional-cuisine.md, and magical-pedagogy.md to serve as briefs for future visual assets, and integrating this new lore into military and character documents.
  • System Hardening & Asset Creation: Completed multiple cycles focused on repository maintenance and polish. Key activities included refining the project's style-guide.md, creating symbolic briefs for all 22 Paths, refactoring several generation scripts for better maintainability, converting numerous inline SVGs to static assets, and performing comprehensive prose and style audits on foundational documents like "The Wanderer's Path." Added several new visual assets for key concepts and narrative scenes (Oracle's Contract, Chokmah Star-Moths, Un-naming Ritual).
  • Core Concept Visualization: Fulfilled several high-priority visual design goals by creating and integrating diagrams for core concepts, including The Three Pillars, Strategic Doctrines, The Qliphoth Tree, and a comprehensive Cosmology Overview, improving the accessibility of foundational lore.
  • Faction Aesthetics: Created and integrated new visual assets to better define factional identity, including diagrams for architectural-styles.md and factional-cuisine.md.
  • Iconography: Generated a complete set of 10 symbolic icons for the Qliphothic Husks and integrated them into the core cosmology documents, providing a clear visual language for these concepts.
  • Narrative Visualization: Created and integrated a new diagram for "The Absurdity Offensive" narrative case study, fulfilling a high-priority visual design goal.
  • Content Restructuring & Style Audit: Executed a major review and restructuring pass to improve clarity and conciseness. Merged scattered documents on factional culture (magical-pedagogy, architecture, cuisine) into a single factional-sociology file. Consolidated all character profiles (master-caelus, oracle-of-the-glass-lens, somnus, key-figures-of-nexus) into the main character-exemplars document. Merged and shortened the three core factional viewpoints. Rewrote and condensed several long narrative texts to better align with the project's style guide. Continued this pass, further shortening narrative texts (folklore-and-superstition, factional-case-studies-overview) and in-world treatises to be more direct and less pretentious. Merged the battle-of-the-serene-valley and battle-of-the-glass-plains documents into a single, more concise case study.
  • Content Review & Consolidation: Executed a review pass focused on conciseness and style guide adherence. Shortened several in-world texts (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, the-mage-as-an-engine) to have a more direct and less pretentious tone. Merged and condensed clash-of-doctrines-concord-vs-garden into factional-case-studies-overview. Merged the three core factional viewpoints from foundational-texts into the-three-pillars-overview and deleted the redundant files. This continues the effort to align narrative content with the project's wiki-documentation style.
  • Manual Catalog Curation: Removed the obsolete catalog generation scripts. The catalog files in docs/catalog/ are now manually curated. As part of this change, the catalog tables were significantly shortened to improve their function as at-a-glance summaries. Narrative documents like folklore-and-superstition and in-world treatises were also edited to be more concise and less pretentious, aligning them with the project style guide.
  • System Expansion & Content Polish: Performed a review pass to shorten and rewrite several in-world texts (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, the-mage-as-an-engine, advanced-magical-diagnostics) for conciseness and adherence to the style guide. Merged the-working-mage.md into factional-sociology.md to consolidate lore. Expanded the core magic system by adding a detailed breakdown of 'Build Tall' vs. 'Build Wide' progression philosophies to milestones-and-progression.md, increasing the depth of Awakened power scaling.
  • System Expansion & Content Polish: Performed a review pass to shorten and rewrite several in-world texts (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, techno-magical-symbiosis, the-mage-as-an-engine, advanced-magical-diagnostics) for conciseness and adherence to the style guide. Expanded the core magic system by adding a detailed breakdown of 'Build Tall' vs. 'Build Wide' progression philosophies to milestones-and-progression.md, increasing the depth of Awakened power scaling. Created and integrated new visual assets for mage_engine_diagram.svg and unnaming_ritual.svg.
  • Review & Asset Creation: Executed a review pass focused on conciseness and style guide adherence. Shortened several narrative and treatise documents (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, the-mage-as-an-engine, techno-magical-symbiosis, advanced-magical-diagnostics, infiltration-of-the-warring-kingdom). Merged unawakened-philosophies.md into guilds-and-professions.md to consolidate Unawakened lore. Created and integrated a new visual asset for unnaming_ritual.svg.
  • System Expansion & Style Polish: Performed a review pass to shorten several narrative and lore documents (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, infiltration-of-the-warring-kingdom, factional-sociology) for conciseness and adherence to the style guide. Expanded the core magic system by adding a detailed breakdown of 'Build Tall' vs. 'Build Wide' progression philosophies to milestones-and-progression.md, including the "Breaking Point (Tier 5)" concept to increase the depth of Awakened power scaling.
  • Iconography & System Expansion: Executed a major review and asset creation pass. Shortened several in-world texts (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks, all Qliphoth lore files) for conciseness. Expanded the core magic system by adding a detailed breakdown of 'Build Tall' vs. 'Build Wide' progression philosophies and the "Breaking Point (Tier 5)" concept to milestones-and-progression.md. Created and integrated a complete set of 22 symbolic icons for the Arcana, fulfilling a high-priority asset goal and improving visual navigation of the magic system.
  • Content Polish & System Expansion: Executed a major review pass to improve conciseness and adhere to the project style guide. Shortened and rewrote numerous in-world texts (a-divers-journal, the-heresy-of-the-husks), lore lists (progenitor-factions, qliphothic-avatars), and realm/qliphoth primers. Expanded the core magic system by detailing the "Authority vs. Engineering" paradigm for Tall vs. Wide builds in milestones-and-progression.md and added a new non-combat Resonant Cycle (The Poetic Thesis). Created and integrated a new panoramic SVG for the city of Nexus.
  • System Expansion & Asset Creation: Performed a review pass focused on conciseness and style guide adherence, shortening several narrative and lore documents. Expanded the magic system by adding a new Dissonant Cycle (The Unbroken Rampart) to increase build diversity. Created and integrated new SVG assets for Unawakened guild equipment to fulfill visual design goals and deepen mundane faction lore.
  • Link Maintenance & Consolidation: Executed a comprehensive pass to fix over 2000 broken and non-canonical links identified by the bad-links-report.md. This involved updating links to content that had been merged into overview documents (e.g., individual battle reports, character profiles, and sociological studies) and converting all relative wiki links to the canonical, server-absolute format required by the static site generator. This significantly improved repository health and build stability.
  • Link Checker & Fixer Tooling: Improved the developer dashboard's link checker to more accurately detect non-canonical links that break the static site generator (e.g., links to directories without a trailing slash). Added a new "Fix All Links" tool that automatically rewrites all relative or non-canonical links across the entire repository to the required server-absolute format, greatly simplifying link maintenance.
  • Link Fixer & SVG Polish: Improved the "Fix All Links" tool to correctly canonicalize malformed server-absolute links (e.g., those with // or missing trailing slashes). Corrected the positioning of path labels on the Tree of Life diagram for better readability by calculating them dynamically.

Phase 4: Narrative Deepening (Completed)

  • Primary Goal: To build out the major narrative arcs of the K10 world by detailing the key conflicts, events, and characters that define its history. This phase focused on creating interconnected, in-world historical documents rather than broad system design.
  • Key Achievements:
    • Narrative Arc Completion: Substantially fleshed out the "War of the Shifting Throne" arc with numerous detailed historical documents, character backstories, and playtest scenarios, creating a rich, interconnected narrative.
    • System & Lore Finalization: Reached a major project milestone with the completion of all core system documents. This includes:
      • All 22 Arcanum Legion primers.
      • Detailed mechanics for all 22 Paths and their Mastery Invocations.
      • Full breakdowns for all foundational and esoteric magical Cycles.
    • Catalog Integration: Formatted all core magic system documents with summary tables to facilitate the auto-generation of the catalogs in docs/catalog/.
    • Repository Polish: Performed comprehensive reviews and link management passes, ensuring high consistency, correcting errors, and improving document structure and accessibility across the wiki.

Phase 3: System Refinement & Polish (Completed)

  • Primary Goal: To refine, connect, and expand the foundational content from previous phases, adding depth and texture to the world while ensuring consistency. This phase marked the maturation of the core systems and lore.
  • Key Activities:
    • Faction & Military Deepening: Expanded faction military rosters ("Beast-Kin", Unawakened corps), integrated Nexus guild logistics, and completed strategic counters for all 22 Arcanum Legions.
    • Cultural Expansion: Added a comprehensive suite of documents on factional aesthetics, architecture, cuisine, justice systems, and social structures, providing a rich, non-military context for each faction.
    • System Refinement: Added "Invocation" abilities to Arcanum Mastery tiers for more concrete, non-combat progression, and validated esoteric archetypes through numerous narrative playtests.
    • Narrative Arc Foundation: Established the "War of the Shifting Throne" arc and detailed its initial key events, setting the stage for Phase 4.

Phase 2: Content Expansion

  • Primary Goal: To build upon the foundational systems with a wide array of detailed content, adding texture and depth to the world.
  • Key Activities:
    • System & Lore Expansion: Completed the full set of the 22 Arcanum Legions, 10 Qliphothic Avatars, 10 Sefirotic Realms, and numerous Archetypal Builds.
    • RTS Lore Integration: Added specific RTS profiles and tactical doctrines to all military factions to provide a rich background for strategic gameplay concepts.
    • Unawakened Perspectives: Created a library of in-world texts to showcase the Unawakened view on magic, economics, and warfare, adding a crucial non-magical perspective to the world.
    • Narrative Content: Added battle-perspective documents and folkloric tales to illustrate how the core concepts of the world manifest in story and culture.

Phase 1: Core System Foundation

  • Primary Goal: To establish the foundational pillars of the K10 project.
  • Key Activities:
    • Magic System: Wrote the core documents for the 10 Sefirot, the principles of Corruption, the 22 Paths, and the mechanics of Progression and Cycles.
    • Faction Creation: Defined the philosophies, military arms, and core beliefs of the three major factions: the Adamant Concord, the Flowing Garden, and the Way of the Middle Pillar.
    • Cosmology: Outlined the fundamental structure of the K10 universe, including the Ein Sof, the Abyss of Da'at, and the parasitic nature of the Qliphoth.
