K10 Development Plan

This document outlines the roadmap and iteration plan for the writing and development of the K10 world. Please regularly update this file according to the progress of the repo and remove completed tasks.

Phase 5: The Unawakened World (Ongoing)

  • Goals: To significantly deepen the lore and narrative significance of the Unawakened factions, particularly the guilds of Nexus. This phase will shift focus from high-level magical conflicts to the mundane, economic, and social systems that form the bedrock of the K10 world.

Cycle Log

This log tracks the last completed development iteration.

  • Role 4/5 (Maintainer): Improved the link fixing tool on the developer dashboard to more robustly detect and correct non-canonical links (e.g., those missing trailing slashes). Polished the Tree of Life SVG diagram by automatically calculating the position and angle of path labels for improved readability.

Next Iteration Focus: Reviewer / Maintainer (Role 4/5)

With the new link fixing tools in place, the next cycle will focus on repository health and content polish.

  • Task 1 (Primary): Use the new "Fix All Links" tool on the dev dashboard to perform a full canonicalization pass on all repository links. Follow this with a "Run Link Check" to ensure a clean bad-links-report.md.
  • Task 2 (Secondary): Continue the comprehensive review of all documents, focusing on shortening texts, improving prose, and ensuring adherence to the style guide. Pay special attention to longer narrative documents and character histories.

Visual Design Goals (Prioritized)

This list is prioritized based on the project's current focus.

Priority 1: The Unawakened World (Phase 5 Focus) - Nexus & Guilds: - nexus-the-city-of-glass.md: Panoramic cityscape of the three wards. - guilds-and-professions.md: Concept art for guild equipment (Lamplighter's resonance jar, Weaver's Grey Cloak). - Unawakened Culture:

Priority 2: Core Concepts & Factions - Faction Aesthetics: - main-factions-military/: Field-guide style illustrations for unique military units. - Magic System: - the-22-paths.md: A set of 22 simple, iconic symbols for each of the Arcana. - the-mage-as-an-engine.md: A schematic overlaying the Tree of Life on an engine blueprint.

Priority 3: Narrative & Evocative Scenes - Historical Events: - the-absurdity-offensive.md: Scene of rabbits filling a Concordian command tent. - the-glitch-in-the-gilded-cage.md: Conceptual duel between Cassian and Praetor Kael. - the-war-of-the-carrion-king.md: Justicar Roric performing the "Surgeon's Mercy." - Cosmology & Folklore: - folklore-and-superstition.md: Illustration for "The Clockwork Saint" or "The Shattered King." - sefirotic-realms.md: An illustration for an evocative realm like the Clockwork Inevitable or the Lunar Sea.

Summary of Completed Work

This section provides a high-level overview of previously completed work. For a detailed history, please see the full changelog.md.

  • Phase 1 - Core Foundation: Established the core documents for the magic system (10 Sefirot, 22 Paths, Corruption, Cycles), the three major factions, and the world's cosmology.
  • Phase 2 - Content Expansion: Built upon the foundation with detailed content, including the full set of Qliphothic Avatars, Sefirotic Realms, and Archetypal Builds. Added RTS profiles, Unawakened perspectives, and narrative content to deepen the world.
  • Phase 3 - System Refinement & Polish: Focused on deepening existing content and ensuring consistency. Key activities included expanding faction military rosters ("Beast-Kin", Unawakened corps), integrating Nexus guild logistics, adding cultural documents (aesthetics, architecture, cuisine, law), refining the magic system with "Invocations," establishing the "War of the Shifting Throne" arc, and validating esoteric archetypes through numerous playtests.
  • Phase 4 (Milestones): Completed the full set of 22 detailed primers for the Arcanum Legions. Completed the detailed mechanical breakdowns for all 22 Paths and all foundational Cycles.
