Qliphothic Avatars: A Field Guide

A Black-Book Compilation of Known Infestations

Compiler's Warning: These are not practitioners of an Art. They are diseases of the soul, the final monstrous state of a Diver who has succumbed to Infestation. They are walking vectors of cosmic cancer. Do not reason with them. Their logic is poison. The only permissible interaction is termination.

This guide catalogues the 10 monstrous archetypes that result from a Diver's surrender to the Qliphoth, each a living avatar of its patron Husk.

Note: This content is also available in the Factions & Figures Catalog.

1. The Thaumiel Demagogue

  • Core Philosophy: "Oneness is tyranny. Truth is found in conflict. Let them fight."
  • Origin: S1 Keter -> Thaumiel, The Twins of God
  • Manifestations: A perversion of Keter's unity that fosters division.
    • Aura of Contention: Exacerbates discord and curdles trust into suspicion.
    • Forked Tongue: A conceptual attack that convinces a target it has always been two separate, warring entities.
  • Profile: The ultimate social saboteur. Destroys factions from within, but has almost no direct combat ability. Ineffective against a single, self-assured individual; a Tiferet master is nearly immune.
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form may have two overlapping faces or voices. Their paradoxical existence causes reality to destabilize around them.

2. The Ghagiel Deceiver

  • Core Philosophy: "Certainty is a cage. Let me show you a thousand futures, all of them traps."
  • Origin: S2 Chokmah -> Ghagiel, The Hinderers
  • Manifestations: A perversion of Chokmah's insight; an oracle of noise.
    • Aura of Indecision: Floods minds with an endless stream of trivial, contradictory data, paralyzing decision-making.
    • Oracle of Noise: Provides a perfectly detailed and utterly false divination, designed to be acted upon to ruinous effect.
  • Profile: Devastating against strategists and seers. Has almost no direct physical power and is ineffective against beings of pure instinct or overwhelming conviction (e.g., a Netzach Champion).
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form is often a being of cracked mirrors and whispering dust, its face a shifting kaleidoscope of its victims' confused expressions.

3. The Satariel Jailer

  • Core Philosophy: "Hope is the first and cruelest lie. I will give you the peace of a perfect, silent, lightless cage."
  • Origin: S3 Binah -> Satariel, The Concealers
  • Manifestations: Twists Binah's Structure into an art of absolute imprisonment.
    • Ward of Despair: Creates a zone of sensory deprivation and conceptual hopelessness, making escape feel impossible.
    • Construct of Silence: Manifests a perfect, featureless prison of black energy that swallows all light, sound, and magical scrying.
  • Profile: The ultimate control mage, whose prisons attack the will, not the body. Methods are slow and lack immediate destructive power. Countered by overwhelming, irrational passion (Netzach).
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). The Jailer's final form is often a being of perfect, featureless black geometry, a humanoid silhouette of pure void that drains all light, sound, and hope.

4. The Gha'agsheblah Parasite

  • Core Philosophy: "I love you so much I will make you a part of me. We will grow together, forever."
  • Origin: S4 Chesed -> Gha'agsheblah, The Smothering Ones
  • Manifestations: Twists Chesed's giving into a smothering, parasitic "love."
    • Tumorous Touch: A "healing" touch that triggers monstrous, uncontrolled regeneration, turning a target into a cancerous mass of flesh.
    • Parasitic Bond: Creates a metaphysical tether to a victim, drawing sustenance and puppeting their actions.
  • Profile: An expert in area denial and body horror. Power relies on proximity and touch. Conceptual Severance is highly effective against its bonds.
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Physical body becomes a grotesque, bloated mass, inseparable from the garden of horrors it cultivates. Often stationary, acting as the "heart" of its corrupted territory.

5. The Golachab Arsonist

  • Core Philosophy: "Structure is a lie. Judgement is a lie. Only the fire is real. Let it all burn."
  • Origin: S5 Gevurah -> Golachab, The Burners with Fire
  • Manifestations: Inverts Gevurah's Judgment into mindless annihilation.
    • Indiscriminate Fire: Conjures conceptual fire that consumes all things—living, dead, or abstract—with equal ferocity.
    • Annihilating Blow: A focused strike that does not stop until the target is destroyed, often harming the Arsonist through over-exertion.
  • Profile: Immense raw destructive power, but lacks subtlety. Power is draining and self-consuming, and they are easily baited into traps.
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Body is often covered in weeping, self-inflicted burns, its eyes pits of embers. Little more than a living gateway for Golachab's destructive impulse.

6. The Thagirion Tormentor

  • Core Philosophy: "Beauty is a lie. Harmony is a cage. Look in my mirror and I will show you the ugly, screaming truth of what you are."
  • Origin: S6 Tiferet -> Thagirion, The Disputers
  • Manifestations: An avatar of weaponized self-loathing that inverts Tiferet's harmony.
    • Psychic Flay: Amplifies a target's deepest insecurities into a debilitating psychic pain.
    • Pain-Constructs: Manifests a victim's inner flaws as tangible beings of shadow and broken glass that torment them.
  • Profile: Devastating against single individuals with complex psyches, turning a target's mind against them. Ineffective against simple creatures or those without a strong sense of self (e.g., Binah constructs).
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form is often a being of horrific, ostentatious "beauty," a walking parody of a Tiferet adept whose screams of agony are its only communication.

7. The A'arab Zaraq Zealot

  • Core Philosophy: "Victory at any cost. Passion is not a fire to warm the soul, but a raven to consume the world."
  • Origin: S7 Netzach -> A'arab Zaraq, The Ravens of Dispersion
  • Manifestations: Embodies Netzach's passion curdled into despairing hunger.
    • Aura of Futility: Psychically drains the passion and drive from others, causing heroic charges to falter.
    • Swarm of Envy: Manifests psychic ravens that drain a target's will to fight, making any victory taste of ash.
  • Profile: An ultimate "anti-morale" unit, devastating against armies that rely on passion and heroism. Power is a slow drain and can be overwhelmed by cold, logical force (Binah/Gevurah).
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form is often a gaunt, tar-feathered figure, constantly followed by a flock of shadowy ravens.

8. The Samael Sophist

  • Core Philosophy: "Truth is a function of conviction. My argument is more beautiful than yours; therefore, I am right."
  • Origin: S8 Hod -> Samael, the Poison of God
  • Manifestations: A master manipulator who weaponizes beautiful lies.
    • Gilded Argument: Constructs a lie so splendid and internally consistent it can temporarily overwrite a target's perception of reality.
    • Glyph of Conversion: Inscribes glyphs that can rewrite a person's core beliefs on contact.
  • Profile: An unparalleled deceiver and infiltrator. Power is self-destructive and parasitic, warping their minds with solipsistic logic. Universally hunted by all major factions.
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). This is not a risk; it is the inevitable conclusion of their path.
  • Example: The tale of Scribe Xanthus charts the journey of a Hod mage into a Samael Sophist.

9. The Gamaliel Phantasmist

  • Core Philosophy: "Your ugliest thoughts are the most honest part of you. Let me give them shape. Let me show you your true face."
  • Origin: S9 Yesod -> Gamaliel, The Obscene Ones
  • Manifestations: Inverts Yesod's art of illusion into psychological terrorism.
    • Dredge Trauma: Reaches into a target's subconscious to give their most repressed fears, shames, and guilts tangible, horrifying form.
    • Psychic Vampirism: Feeds on the fear and despair their horrors generate.
  • Profile: A devastating psychological warrior who neutralizes targets without physical confrontation. Has very little direct physical power and relies on targets having a complex psyche to exploit. Beings with profound inner harmony (Tiferet masters) are highly resistant.
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form is a being of shifting shadow and whispered obscenities, its form constantly changing to reflect the fears of those who look upon it.

10. The Nahemoth Ghoul-King

  • Core Philosophy: "The spirit is a lie the flesh tells itself. There is only hunger. There is only rot. Let me make you honest."
  • Origin: S10 Malkuth -> Nahemoth, The Whispering Ones
  • Manifestations: Embodies Malkuth's physicality divorced from all higher meaning.
    • Ghoul Animation: Animates the dead as extensions of its will, driven by an insatiable hunger.
    • Aura of Decay: Their presence is a field of accelerated entropy, causing materials to rot and living flesh to necrotize.
  • Profile: Can create a massive, disposable army and is devastating in a battle of attrition. Lacks strategy, relying on overwhelming numbers. Highly vulnerable to conceptual fire (Gevurah) or life-giving energy (Chesed).
  • Corruption Vector: Infestation (Avatar). Final form is often a monstrous, bloated amalgam of corpses and rotted earth, constantly consuming and adding to its own mass.
  • Historical Note: The infamous Ghoul-King of the Dust-Cairns was the central antagonist of the War of the Carrion King.
