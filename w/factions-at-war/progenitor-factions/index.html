Progenitor Factions: The War in Heaven

Archivist's Note: An Ascended mage who achieves Progenitorship does not retire to a private paradise; they become a key figure on the front line of a silent, eternal war. This document is a strategic assessment of the ten "Progenitor Factions"â€”the armies cultivated by Ascended mages within their respective Sefirotic Realms. These are not armies of mortals, but living manifestations of cosmic principles, each locked in a struggle against its own Qliphothic shadow.

1. The Kingmakers (S1 Keter)

  • Doctrine: The Kingmakers do not seek victory for themselves, but to enable it in others. They are catalysts, identifying and empowering champions and concepts they deem worthy. Their warfare is an act of divine investment, making them a powerful support faction on a cosmic scale.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The realm has no native life. A Progenitor's Domain is a forge for enabling potential, creating conceptual "gifts" like Sparks of Potential or Foundries of Souls to empower champions and concepts in other realms.
  • RTS Profile: A unique support faction that buffs allies and unlocks global abilities, winning by enabling victory for others.
    • Key "Units": Spark of Potential (Support), Foundry of Souls (Structure), Avatar of the Point (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The First Emanation: Target an ally. For a short time, they gain access to a powerful, unique technology or unit from another faction's tech tree.

2. The Star-Weavers (S2 Chokmah)

  • Doctrine: The Star-Weavers wage war through perfect foresight. They see all possible timelines and manipulate causality to ensure their chosen future comes to pass. They do not fight battles; they prune the timelines where they lose.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The realm is inhabited by the Star-Weavers, conceptual beings of sentient information who navigate chaotic "Possibility Storms" and spin the webs of causality.
  • RTS Profile: An intelligence and subterfuge faction that focuses on scouting, predicting enemy movements, and setting traps to win before a fight begins.
    • Key "Units": Oracle-Spider (Scout), Probability-Elemental (Debuffer), Causal Agent (Specialist).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Inevitable Timeline: Reveals predicted enemy movement paths and lets you lock one enemy to their predicted path for an ambush.

3. The Cog-Legions (S3 Binah)

  • Doctrine: The Cog-Legions seek to impose a perfect, static, and logical order on all of reality. Conflict is a flaw in the cosmic system, a bug to be corrected. Their advance is slow, methodical, and inexorable, a grinding wave of perfect, sterile geometry.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The realm's automata are not built, but precipitated from its substance when a new logical necessity arises. Key inhabitants include the Form-Wrights (crystalline architects), Logic-Bearers (massive law-enforcers), and Inexorables (sleek hunter-constructs tasked with eliminating paradoxes). Society is the flawless execution of function, a silent, passionless "Great Calculation" to refine the universe.
  • RTS Profile: A slow, defensive "turtle" faction whose expensive but durable units excel at establishing an unbreakable line and grinding down the enemy.
    • Key "Units": Cog-Legionary (Infantry), Form-Golem (Heavy Infantry), Logic-Elemental (Caster).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Final Law: For a short period, all friendly buildings become invulnerable and all active "Rule" debuffs on enemies have their duration doubled.

4. The Symbiotic Tide (S4 Chesed)

  • Doctrine: The Symbiotic Tide wages war as an act of boundless, smothering growth. It does not seek to destroy its enemies, but to absorb and integrate them into its single, massive, super-organism. Its advance is a cancerous, hyper-fertile bloom that turns enemy territory into a part of itself.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: A single, world-sized super-organism where there is no distinction between individual and ecosystem. Its "units" are specialized organs: Spore-Swarms act as an immune system, Warden-Beasts act as mobile healers and shepherds, and colossal Bio-Titans act as living fortresses and spawning grounds. Its purpose is singular: to grow, assimilate, and transform everything into a part of its unified whole.
  • RTS Profile: A "Zerg"-like faction focused on rapid expansion and overwhelming numbers. Its units are weaker but possess passive regeneration and can be produced quickly.
    • Key "Units": Spore-Swarm (Swarm Infantry), Warden-Beast (Healer), Bio-Titan (Heavy Unit).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Great Outpouring: Heals all units on the map (friend and foe) to full health and gives a massive temporary regeneration boost to friendly units.

5. The Ashen Host (S5 Gevurah)

  • Doctrine: The Ashen Host believes that existence is a crucible, a constant test of strength and worth. War is not a means to an end, but the ultimate act of judgment. They seek to find and destroy weakness wherever it existsâ€”in their enemies, and more importantly, in themselves.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Ashen Host, a feudal society of sentient warriors in volcanic armor. Their culture is a constant trial by combat to test strength and destroy weakness, both in their enemies and themselves.
  • RTS Profile: An elite, low-model-count faction with expensive, powerful units focused on precision strikes and exploiting enemy weaknesses.
    • Key "Units": Initiate (Training Unit), Judicator (Elite Infantry), Ashen Knight (Hero-Killer).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Crucible: Creates a large arena of fire where all units inside are forced to attack the nearest enemy and have their damage significantly increased.

6. The Golden Choir (S6 Tiferet)

  • Doctrine: The Golden Choir does not wage war; it resolves it. Conflict is a form of spiritual sickness, a dissonance in the song of the cosmos. Their goal is to project a harmony so perfect and absolute that violence becomes a conceptual impossibility.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Golden Choir, beings of pure harmony and light that are more living music than biological life. Each "Aion of Virtue" is a sentient construct embodying an ideal like 'Compassion' or 'Sacrifice'.
  • RTS Profile: A non-lethal "pacification" faction. Its units apply "Harmony" debuffs that eventually convert enemies into neutral units.
    • Key "Units": Emissary of Serenity (Caster), Harmonious Construct (Defensive Aura), Avatar of the Self (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Great Centering: For a short period, the "Harmony" meter of all enemy units on the map fills at a massively accelerated rate.

7. The Unconquered Host (S7 Netzach)

  • Doctrine: The Unconquered Host exists for the glory of the struggle. For them, war is the ultimate expression of passion and endurance. Victory is not an outcome, but a continuous act of striving and overcoming. An eternal charge against an infinite foe.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Wild Host, bestial humanoids who embody the glory of the hunt. Their society is a series of nomadic war-bands locked in a constant struggle for dominance and glory.
  • RTS Profile: A momentum-based aggressive faction. Its units gain powerful, permanent "veterancy" buffs for every enemy they kill.
    • Key "Units": War-Band Skirmisher (Core Infantry), Victory Banner (Support), Champion of the Hunt (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Unending Charge: Grants all friendly units a massive boost to movement speed and attack damage, refreshed and increased for any unit that gets a kill.

8. The Glistening Assembly (S8 Hod)

  • Doctrine: The Glistening Assembly wages war as an act of information supremacy. They do not destroy armies; they bankrupt them of options. They win by rewriting the enemy's strategic manual, turning their strengths into weaknesses through perfect, splendidly complex illusions and logical traps.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Glistening Assembly, a civilization of sentient information. Its inhabitants are data-golems and logic-elementals whose society is a perfect, logical network where status is determined by the elegance of one's code.
  • RTS Profile: A high-tech subterfuge faction that creates illusory units, disables enemy abilities, and wins through superior information and control.
    • Key "Units": Data-Sprite (Scout/Harasser), Glamour Projector (Caster), Logic-Elemental (Caster).
    • Ultimate Ability: System Crash: For a short time, all enemy units and structures have their special abilities and production queues completely disabled.

9. The Phantasmal Flotilla (S9 Yesod)

  • Doctrine: The Flotilla understands that the foundation of any army is belief. Their warfare is an act of psychological erosion, turning an enemy's subconscious against them. They do not kill soldiers; they manifest their deepest fears and watch as their enemies' morale shatters.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Phantasmal Flotilla, beings of dream-stuff who sail the Lunar Sea on ships of solidified memory. Their society is composed of living archetypes, myths, and legends.
  • RTS Profile: A morale-breaking faction. Its units apply powerful "Fear" debuffs that reduce enemy combat effectiveness or cause them to flee.
    • Key "Units": Phantasmal Agent (Stealth Debuffer), Dream-Eater (Caster), Manifested Archetype (Summon).
    • Ultimate Ability: Shared Nightmare: All enemy units on the map are instantly afflicted with "Fear" and will flee from combat for a few seconds.

10. The Sovereign Host (S10 Malkuth)

  • Doctrine: The Sovereign Host is the ultimate expression of adaptable, conventional warfare. As masters of the perfected physical realm, they can manifest armies of any elemental composition, perfectly countering any foe. Their doctrine is one of perfect response; for every problem, they manifest the perfect, tangible solution.
  • Inhabitants/Structure: The Sovereign Host, the elements of the world given form and consciousness. A Progenitor does not raise an army but awakens it from the landscape itself, creating a society with no distinction between citizen and state.
  • RTS Profile: A versatile "jack-of-all-trades" faction focused on elemental combined arms, able to adapt its unit composition to counter any threat.
    • Key "Units": Earth Golem (Tank), Wind Spirit (Harasser), Fire Elemental (Ranged DPS), Water Weaver (Healer).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Sovereign's Command: For a short time, the player can directly manipulate the terrain, creating walls of stone, rivers of mud, or fissures of fire.
