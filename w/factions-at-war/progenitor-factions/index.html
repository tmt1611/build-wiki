Progenitor Factions: The War in Heaven

Archivist's Note: An Ascended mage who achieves Progenitorship becomes a key figure in a silent, eternal war. This document is a strategic assessment of the ten "Progenitor Factions"—armies cultivated by Ascended mages within their respective Sefirotic Realms. These are not armies of mortals, but living manifestations of cosmic principles.

1. The Kingmakers (S1 Keter)

  • Doctrine: Enable victory in others. The Kingmakers are catalysts who identify and empower worthy champions and concepts. Their warfare is an act of divine investment.
  • Structure: A Progenitor's Domain in Keter is a forge for enabling potential. It creates conceptual "gifts"—Sparks of Potential or Foundries of Souls—to empower champions in other realms.
  • RTS Profile: A unique support faction that buffs allies and unlocks global abilities.
    • Key "Units": Spark of Potential (Support), Foundry of Souls (Structure), Avatar of the Point (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The First Emanation: For a short time, an ally gains access to a powerful, unique technology or unit from another faction's tech tree.

2. The Star-Weavers (S2 Chokmah)

  • Doctrine: War through perfect foresight. They see all possible timelines and manipulate causality to ensure their chosen future comes to pass. They do not fight battles; they prune the timelines where they lose.
  • Structure: The realm is inhabited by the Star-Weavers, conceptual beings of sentient information who navigate chaotic "Possibility Storms" and spin the webs of causality.
  • RTS Profile: An intelligence and subterfuge faction focused on scouting, predicting enemy movements, and setting traps.
    • Key "Units": Oracle-Spider (Scout), Probability-Elemental (Debuffer), Causal Agent (Specialist).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Inevitable Timeline: Reveals predicted enemy movement paths and lets you lock one enemy to their predicted path for an ambush.

3. The Cog-Legions (S3 Binah)

  • Doctrine: Impose a perfect, static, and logical order on all of reality. Conflict is a flaw in the cosmic system, a bug to be corrected. Their advance is a slow, grinding wave of sterile geometry.
  • Structure: The realm's automata are not built, but precipitated from its substance when a new logical necessity arises. Key inhabitants include Form-Wrights (architects), Logic-Bearers (law-enforcers), and Inexorables (paradox-hunters).
  • RTS Profile: A slow, defensive "turtle" faction with expensive, durable units that excel at establishing an unbreakable line.
    • Key "Units": Cog-Legionary (Infantry), Form-Golem (Heavy Infantry), Logic-Elemental (Caster).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Final Law: For a short period, all friendly buildings become invulnerable and active "Rule" debuffs on enemies have their duration doubled.

4. The Symbiotic Tide (S4 Chesed)

  • Doctrine: War as an act of boundless, smothering growth. It does not seek to destroy enemies, but to absorb and integrate them into its single super-organism. Its advance is a cancerous, hyper-fertile bloom.
  • Structure: A single, world-sized super-organism. Its "units" are specialized organs: Spore-Swarms (immune system), Warden-Beasts (mobile healers), and colossal Bio-Titans (living fortresses).
  • RTS Profile: A "Zerg"-like faction focused on rapid expansion and overwhelming numbers. Units are weaker but possess passive regeneration.
    • Key "Units": Spore-Swarm (Swarm Infantry), Warden-Beast (Healer), Bio-Titan (Heavy Unit).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Great Outpouring: Heals all units on the map (friend and foe) to full health and gives a massive temporary regeneration boost to friendly units.

5. The Ashen Host (S5 Gevurah)

  • Doctrine: Existence is a crucible. War is the ultimate act of judgment. They seek to find and destroy weakness wherever it exists—in their enemies, and more importantly, in themselves.
  • Structure: The Ashen Host is a feudal society of sentient warriors in volcanic armor. Their culture is a constant trial by combat to test strength and destroy weakness.
  • RTS Profile: An elite, low-model-count faction with expensive, powerful units focused on precision strikes.
    • Key "Units": Initiate (Training Unit), Judicator (Elite Infantry), Ashen Knight (Hero-Killer).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Crucible: Creates a large arena of fire where all units inside are forced to attack the nearest enemy and have their damage significantly increased.

6. The Golden Choir (S6 Tiferet)

  • Doctrine: Resolve conflict, do not wage it. Conflict is a spiritual sickness. Their goal is to project a harmony so perfect that violence becomes a conceptual impossibility.
  • Structure: The Golden Choir are beings of pure harmony and light, more living music than biological life. Each "Aion of Virtue" is a sentient construct embodying an ideal like 'Compassion' or 'Sacrifice'.
  • RTS Profile: A non-lethal "pacification" faction. Its units apply "Harmony" debuffs that eventually convert enemies into neutral units.
    • Key "Units": Emissary of Serenity (Caster), Harmonious Construct (Defensive Aura), Avatar of the Self (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Great Centering: For a short period, the "Harmony" meter of all enemy units on the map fills at a massively accelerated rate.

7. The Unconquered Host (S7 Netzach)

  • Doctrine: The glory of the struggle. War is the ultimate expression of passion and endurance. Victory is not an outcome, but a continuous act of striving and overcoming.
  • Structure: The Wild Host are bestial humanoids who embody the glory of the hunt. Their society is a series of nomadic war-bands locked in a constant struggle for dominance and glory.
  • RTS Profile: A momentum-based aggressive faction. Its units gain powerful, permanent "veterancy" buffs for every enemy they kill.
    • Key "Units": War-Band Skirmisher (Core Infantry), Victory Banner (Support), Champion of the Hunt (Hero).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Unending Charge: Grants all friendly units a massive boost to movement speed and attack damage, refreshed and increased for any unit that gets a kill.

8. The Glistening Assembly (S8 Hod)

  • Doctrine: War as information supremacy. They do not destroy armies, but bankrupt them of options, turning their strengths into weaknesses through illusions and logical traps.
  • Structure: A civilization of sentient information. Its inhabitants are data-golems and logic-elementals whose society is a logical network where status is determined by the elegance of one's code.
  • RTS Profile: A high-tech subterfuge faction that creates illusory units, disables enemy abilities, and wins through superior information.
    • Key "Units": Data-Sprite (Scout/Harasser), Glamour Projector (Caster), Logic-Elemental (Caster).
    • Ultimate Ability: System Crash: For a short time, all enemy units and structures have their special abilities and production queues completely disabled.

9. The Phantasmal Flotilla (S9 Yesod)

  • Doctrine: Warfare as psychological erosion. They do not kill soldiers; they manifest their deepest fears and watch as their enemies' morale shatters.
  • Structure: Beings of dream-stuff who sail the Lunar Sea on ships of solidified memory. Their society is composed of living archetypes, myths, and legends.
  • RTS Profile: A morale-breaking faction. Its units apply powerful "Fear" debuffs that reduce enemy combat effectiveness or cause them to flee.
    • Key "Units": Phantasmal Agent (Stealth Debuffer), Dream-Eater (Caster), Manifested Archetype (Summon).
    • Ultimate Ability: Shared Nightmare: All enemy units on the map are instantly afflicted with "Fear" and will flee from combat for a few seconds.

10. The Sovereign Host (S10 Malkuth)

  • Doctrine: The ultimate expression of adaptable, conventional warfare. As masters of the perfected physical realm, they manifest armies of any elemental composition to perfectly counter any foe.
  • Structure: The elements of the world given form and consciousness. A Progenitor does not raise an army but awakens it from the landscape, creating a society with no distinction between citizen and state.
  • RTS Profile: A versatile "jack-of-all-trades" faction focused on elemental combined arms.
    • Key "Units": Earth Golem (Tank), Wind Spirit (Harasser), Fire Elemental (Ranged DPS), Water Weaver (Healer).
    • Ultimate Ability: The Sovereign's Command: For a short time, the player can directly manipulate the terrain, creating walls, rivers of mud, or fissures of fire.
