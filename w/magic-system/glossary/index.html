Glossary

This document is the single source of truth for all project-specific terms and shorthand.

Ein Sof

(Lit. "The Endless") The infinite, unknowable source of all reality and magical energy. Mana flows from the Ein Sof into the Sefirot. For more, see the Cosmology Overview.

K10

The formal name for the magic system. The "K" references Kabbalah, and "10" refers to the ten Sefirot that form the basis of all magic.

Sefirah (pl. Sefirot)

One of ten divine emanations or cosmic filters that step down the infinite energy of the Ein Sof into distinct, usable concepts and powers. Each Sefirah is a "pathway" of magic. For a visual schematic, see the Tree of Life.

Pathway (S1-S10)

A common term for a Sefirah, referring to the path of progression a mage follows (e.g., S1 for Keter, S2 for Chokmah, etc.).

Cross-Path Facilitation (XPY)

The process of using mastery in one pathway (X) to learn an adjacent one (Y). A core mechanic for "Build Wide" mages detailed in the Milestones & Progression guide.

Milestone

A significant threshold in a mage's development within a pathway, granting new abilities and often resulting in permanent physical or metaphysical changes. See Milestones & Progression.

Da'at

(Lit. "Knowledge") The apocryphal, hidden "non-pathway" located in the Abyss between the supernal Sefirot and the lower seven. It is not a true Sefirah but a conduit to non-existence. Accessing it is forbidden and leads to Annihilation Corruption. See Da'at: The Abyss.

The Abyss

The conceptual void that separates S1/S2/S3 from the other Sefirot. It is the "location" of Da'at and represents the ultimate trial of a mage's spiritual integrity. See Da'at: The Abyss.

Build Tall

A style of magical training focused on achieving the highest possible milestones in a single pathway, common to the Adamant Concord.

Build Wide

A style of magical training focused on opening and developing multiple pathways, often to enable complex circulations. Common to the Flowing Garden.

Magic-Vision

The ability to perceive the flow of mana, magical constructs, and the auras of mages. It is a specific skill that must be learned, often associated with S2 (Chokmah) or S8 (Hod).

Arcanum

The specific art of transformation between two Sefirot. Each of the 22 Paths is a distinct Arcanum. Mastery reflects a mage's wisdom, as opposed to the raw power measured by Sefirotic Milestones.

The 22 Paths

The archetypal channels that connect the Sefirot. Mastering these transformational arts is the core of the "Build Wide" philosophy and enables the creation of complex Cycles. See The 22 Paths: Arts of Transformation.

Insight

The first tier of Arcanum Mastery. A moment of realization about the relationship between two Sefirot, required to use a Path in a Cycle. See The 22 Paths: Arts of Transformation.

Qliphoth (sing. Qliphah)

(Lit. "Husks" or "Shells") The cancerous, parasitic shadows of the Sefirot. Each Qliphah is the extreme, unbalanced form of a Sefirotic concept. They are not a source of power, but of Infestation Corruption. See The Qliphoth.

Diver

A heretical mage who willingly studies and attempts to draw power from the Qliphoth. See The Qliphoth.

Cycle

A self-sustaining magical "engine" in the soul, created by looping mana through multiple Sefirot. Cycles create complex, ongoing effects and are the foundation of all stable multi-Sefirot magic. See Cycles.
