Advanced Magical Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostics is the art of identifying the root cause of a magical affliction, rather than just its surface symptoms. Standard magical sight (Chokmah's raw data or Hod's formatted analysis) observes the current state of a magical system, but fails when confronted with layered or parasitic phenomena.

These principles were developed from lessons learned during the Blighted Heart of the Garden incident.

Diagnostic Techniques

1. Conceptual Triangulation

This core technique requires viewing an affliction through the lenses of opposing conceptual frameworks (Mercy, Severity, Balance) to find contradictions that reveal the root cause.

  • The Chesed Sense: How does this affliction respond to compassion? A paradoxical reaction, such as a blight being fed by healing energy, points to a deeper issue.
  • The Gevurah Sense: What is the core judgment or flaw? This can reveal faint, parasitic signatures hidden beneath the primary symptom.
  • The Tiferet Sense: What is the state of the core self? This can distinguish between similar symptoms, such as the hollowness of Hollowing versus the cancerous growth of Dissolution.

Case Study: In the Blighted Heart, the visible symptom was Dissolution (Mercy). Triangulation revealed it felt hollow (Balance) and carried a parasitic signature of Judgment (Severity). This pointed to the true cause: a parasitic Gevurah effect was causing Hollowing, which in turn caused the victim's own Chesed abilities to run wild.

2. Auric Stratigraphy

This technique treats a corrupted aura as archaeological layers to be excavated. - Surface Layer (Symptom): The most obvious effect (e.g., Dissolution). - Mid-Layer (Mechanism): The direct cause (e.g., the victim's own magic running wild). - Bedrock Layer (Root Cause): The original wound or influence (e.g., a parasitic link).

Standard Magic-Vision sees only the surface. Advanced diagnostics requires looking for deeper layers.

3. Resonance Testing

An active diagnostic technique. The practitioner projects a tiny, pure pulse of a specific Sefirah's energy and observes the reaction. - Resonant Amplification: If the pulse amplifies the affliction, it confirms alignment with that Sefirah. - Dissonant Rejection: If the pulse is violently rejected, it suggests the affliction is antithetical to that Sefirah. - Sympathetic Resonance: The most telling. If the pulse resonates with a hidden, deeper layer, it can reveal the root cause. A pulse of Gevurah in the Blighted Heart case would have resonated with the hidden parasitic cord, revealing its presence.
