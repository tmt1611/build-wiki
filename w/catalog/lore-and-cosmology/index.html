The 10 Sefirotic Realms

The archetypal planes of existence tied to each Sefirah. For full details, see the main The Sefirotic Realms: Worlds Within Worlds document.

Realm Description
The Silent Throne A singularity of pure potential; a uniform, radiant white space representing the precipice of all creation.
The Perfected Earth The physical world, but idealized and under the absolute command of its Progenitor. Mountains rise and rivers of molten gold flow at their whim.
The Unwritten Library An infinite library where shelves of starlight hold shimmering, unwritten timelines. The air hums with the static of pure information.
The Clockwork Inevitable Stark, brutalist geometry of obsidian and brass. The sky is a perfect star-chart, and the ground is a vast, interlocking mechanism that slowly, constantly reconfigures itself towards a more perfect state.
The Boundless Garden A hyper-fertile, Edenic jungle of impossible colors. Rivers of pure life-energy flow between continent-sized trees whose canopies form the sky.
The Crucible of Judgement A volcanic wasteland of black iron under a permanent crimson twilight. The landscape is littered with the shattered remains of things that failed the Crucible's test of strength.
The Resplendent Heart Serene landscapes of flowing golden light, floating crystalline structures, and perfectly symmetrical gardens. The world hums with a constant, gentle music.
The Endless Dawn A vast, untamed wilderness of vibrant plains and dramatic mountains under a perpetual, glorious dawn or dusk. The air hums with untamed energy.
The Crystal Web A city of pure information, made of crystalline structures, glowing circuitry, and holographic architecture. The "sky" is a visualization of the flow of data.
The Lunar Sea An ocean of liquid twilight under a sky of shifting moons. Floating on this sea are islands of solidified dream-stuff and psychic coral reefs.
